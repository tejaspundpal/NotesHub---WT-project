    Explore Notes of CSE

    Here you will get all subject notes of Computer Science and Engineering branch of DKTE college.Also you will get the way to explore different domains like Competitive Programming,Web Development and Data Science.


    Academic years

    Engineering is course of 4 years from which 2nd,3rd and last year are of core subjects.

    Second Year

    Second year give you the taste of the Computer Science and this is the year where you have to build your base of programming and other cool stuff.You need to expolre new things and find your interest and starting work on it.Good luck..!!!

    Third Year

    Third year gives you more knowledge of core subjects like OS,DBMS,Machine Learning etc.You need to make strong DSA and Problem solving skills.Also this the time where you have to prepare for getting Internship and Placement.

    Final Year

    From starting of your final year Companies comes to college for Placement so you should have strong aptitude,programming skills and core subject knowledge.Also you have to complete one mega project in this year.
